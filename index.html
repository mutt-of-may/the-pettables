About The Pettables

The Pettables is a counseling and support agency created to address the structural, creative, and direction-related challenges faced by online creators. We support anyone who creates content—whether that’s video, streaming, art, music, games, or literature—regardless of experience level or financial situation.
Our purpose is to provide creators with equal footing and real opportunities to grow. Many creators want to entertain, express themselves, or eventually earn a living from their work, but struggle due to lack of guidance, resources, or starting capital. We exist to remove those barriers.
We do not control your content, brand, or creative direction, and we do not own any part of your identity or work. Instead, our counselors work alongside you to help externalize, articulate, and organize your goals, ideas, and interests. We help you think clearly about what you want to make, why you want to make it, and how to move forward—while teaching you how to become independent and self-sustaining.
Counselors can assist with planning, research, project organization, content strategy, collaboration coordination, moderation, scheduling, merchandising, and other creator needs. Each counselor is given a budget per talent to help cover brand- or project-related expenses such as equipment, software, games, tools, or other resources that support your creative work.
Our first goal is to help creators grow, learn, and express themselves effectively. Profit is a secondary goal, not a prerequisite. Whether you’re just starting out or refining an existing presence, The Pettables is here to support you—without taking control.
