About The Pettables

The Pettables is an agency created to address the issues VTubers have with the "business direction" of other agencies. We provide structural support as well as financial support for anyone who needs or wants it, regardless of whether or not they have money to give back to us!

Our goal is to provide equal footing to all VTubers, so everyone gets a chance to be seen and heard. We understand that many people who become VTubers are wanting to either make a profit or entertain others, and struggle to do so because they don't know where or how to start, or can't afford to.

We don't control any part of the process, and we don't own your brand or personality, we only provide tools and assistance for using those tools. Our first goal is to help entertain others and give VTubers the chance they are looking for, profit being our second goal.

The things we help with are managing content, collabs, moderation, scheduling, merch, and anything else a VTuber could possibly want or need.

We also provide funding for lower-cost equipment, games, and other things that fit your brand!
